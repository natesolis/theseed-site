# Privacy Policy for The Seed

**Last updated:** [DATE]

The Seed ("the app," "we," "us") is a faith and emotional-reflection app. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. We've written it in plain language. Please read it alongside the actual behavior of the app — if you change what the app collects, update this policy to match.

## The short version

Your journal entries and saved verses stay on your device. The app does not ask for your name, email, or account, and it does not sell your data or show ads. The only information that leaves your device is an anonymous sign-in identifier created by Firebase (described below).

## Information stored on your device

- **Journal reflections** you write
- **Favorite verses** you save
- **Your display name**, if you choose to enter one in Profile
- **App settings**, such as whether daily reminders are turned on

This information is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to us. If you delete the app, this information is removed with it.

## Information processed by third parties

**Firebase Authentication (Google LLC).** When you open the app, it signs you in anonymously through Firebase. This creates a random, anonymous identifier so the app is ready for optional cloud features in the future. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or contact information, and we do not use it to track you across other apps or websites. Firebase's handling of this data is governed by Google's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

> If you remove the unused Firebase modules (Analytics, Crashlytics, Firestore) before release — recommended in the submission checklist — this section remains accurate, because only Authentication is used. If you keep Crashlytics, add a sentence here noting that anonymous crash diagnostics may be collected to help fix bugs.

## Notifications

If you turn on Daily Seed reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device at noon and in the evening. These are generated on your device. You can turn them off anytime in the Profile tab or in your device's Settings.

## Children's privacy

The Seed is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require an account or any personal details to use.

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location.
- We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.
- We do not display third-party advertising.

## Your choices

- You can delete any journal entry or favorite verse inside the app at any time.
- You can disable reminders at any time.
- You can remove all app data by deleting the app from your device.

## Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles information, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above.
