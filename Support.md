## Frequently asked questions

**How do I plant a seed?**
Open the Plant tab, choose the category that fits how you feel, pick the feeling closest to your heart, read the verse and reflection, then write your journal entry and tap "Plant This Seed."

**Where are my reflections saved?**
On your device. Your journal entries and favorite verses are private to you and are not uploaded anywhere.

**How do I save a verse?**
Tap the heart next to any verse. Saved verses appear in the Verses tab.

**How do I turn reminders on or off?**
Go to the Profile tab and toggle "Daily Seed Reminders." You can also manage notification permission in your device's Settings under The Seed.

**How do I delete a reflection?**
Open the reflection from My Garden or the Growth Timeline and tap "Delete This Seed," or swipe to delete in the timeline list.

**Will I lose my data if I delete the app?**
Yes. Because everything is stored privately on your device, deleting the app removes your reflections and saved verses. Cloud backup may come in a future update.

**Which Bible translation do you use?**
The verses are a curated selection chosen to be gentle and encouraging. Translation and version options may expand in future updates.
